How to Calculate BMI

Measurement Units

a.) Metric System (Kgs and M)

Formula:  weight(kg) / ((height * height)(m))

b.) Other System (Lbs and In)

Formula: weight(lb) / ((height * height)(In)) * 703


BMI Categories

<18.5  -> Underweight
18.5 - 24.9  -> Healthy Weight
25.0 - 29.9  -> Overweight
30.0>  -> Obesity


Note:
These calculations are meant for adults
